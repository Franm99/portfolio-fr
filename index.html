About Me

I was raised in the south of Spain, with a humble family that passed on to me the most important thing a person can possess: their values. Since I was a child, I've always had a great interest in how things work, and our ability to create new things out from nothing.

I've never had a mentor in my career, as no one around me liked technology. However, I've never been afraid of making decisions or failing.

To be part on such a wide range of tech branches (robotics, automation, machine learning, computer vision, software testing, data science and web development) has given me the ability to adapt to the unknown.

It is also really enriching to work in such different contexts, groups and cultures. I really think that empathy and communication are the most important skills that everyone should develop, not only for a job, but for their own life.

After the last years, I still do not have a prefered position in the tech world. But what I'm sure about, is that I'll always be committed to learning and discovering. For me, programming is about resolving puzzles and riddles in the hardest way someone could imagine.

But everyone needs goals. And our goals change over time. So, right now, for me it is to find the best way to help those that are afraid of the power of technology. Any way of making it enjoyable for that kind of people, that is what I want to be part of. How? Let's free our minds, and maybe, work together!😄
